Business Development Executive

Job Description

Company Description

Densible is seeking a motivated Business Development Executive to lead our business expansion efforts. The successful candidate will play a pivotal role in identifying new business opportunities, building relationships with clients, and driving the growth of Densible clientele. This role offers the chance to work in a dynamic and supportive environment, contribute to the company's growth.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Identify and target potential clients, generate high-quality leads through diverse sources such as market research, online bidding platforms, email marketing, LinkedIn, networking, cold calling, and digital marketing strategies to expand the client base.
  2. Manage and optimize company profiles on platforms like Up work, LinkedIn, and other industry-specific platforms to enhance visibility and engagement.
  3. Devise and implement effective bidding strategies to secure projects.
  4. Interact with clients on bidding platforms to clarify project requirements and negotiate terms.
  5. Develop persuasive, tailored proposals outlining our services, solutions, and value proposition, and create, maintain, and update portfolios showcasing our company's capabilities, projects, and success stories for potential clients.
  6. Conduct market research to recognize trends, competitors, and growth opportunities.
  7. Collaborate with marketing and development teams to align strategies.
  8. Lead and supervise a team of online bidding professionals, offering guidance, training, and performance evaluations.
  9. Create detailed proposals and quotes, emphasizing the benefits and value of our solutions.

Qualifications:

  1. Bachelor's degree in business, computer science, or related field.
  2. 1 to 3 years of proven business development executive experience.
  3. Proficiency in email marketing campaigns, cold calling, online bidding platforms, LinkedIn, and other pertinent business development platforms.
  4. Exceptional communication and negotiation skills.
